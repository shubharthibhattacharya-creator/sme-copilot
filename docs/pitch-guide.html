Section 01

How to Open the Conversation

Do not open with features. Open with a problem they feel every single month.

"How much time does your team spend every month chasing clients for documents, manually checking who has filed and who hasn't, and sending WhatsApp reminders one by one?"

Most CA principals will say 30–40% of staff time. Some will laugh and say more. That moment — when they admit the number out loud — is your opening. Everything after that is showing them the solution to a problem they just confirmed they have.

What You Show Them — In This Order

  • 1
    The GST Filing Calendar One screen. All clients. Colour-coded. Green = filed. Red = overdue. Amber = documents missing. No spreadsheet, no calling around, no "let me check."
  • 2
    Bulk Document Request via WhatsApp Select 10 overdue clients. Click Request Documents. They each get a WhatsApp with a link. They upload from their phone. Files appear in your dashboard, tagged to that client and period.
  • 3
    Collections — One-Click Reminders Every client who owes fees is ranked by risk. One click sends a payment reminder over WhatsApp. The system enforces a gap — clients are followed up consistently, not spammed.
  • 4
    AI Compliance Assistant Ask it "what documents does a composition dealer need for annual return?" It answers in 10 seconds from your firm's own SOPs. New staff stop making mistakes. Senior staff stop answering the same questions.
  • 5
    GSTIN Validation Add a client — we check their GSTIN with the GST portal in real time. Cancelled registration? Red alert immediately, before you waste time preparing the return.
Demo Rule: Show Only 3 Things A demo that takes 45 minutes loses deals. Show the filings screen, the document request flow, and the GSTIN validation. Three things. Let them ask about the rest.

Section 04

Objections — Handle These Before They Come Up

❝ We already use ClearTax / Tally / spreadsheets ❞
OpsCopilot does not replace those. It sits on top. ClearTax files the return — OpsCopilot tracks whether the client sent you the documents to file it in the first place. They work together, not against each other.
❝ Our clients are not tech-savvy, they won't use a link ❞
They already use WhatsApp all day. The upload link opens in a browser — no login, no app install. If a 60-year-old business owner can forward a PDF on WhatsApp, they can use this.
❝ What happens to our client data? ❞
Answer this before they ask. Data is stored in encrypted servers. Each firm's data is completely isolated — no data is shared with third parties. We are not ClearTax — we do not use your client data to build competing products.
❝ We are a small firm, we can manage manually ❞
The problem is not whether you can manage manually today. It is that manual does not scale. When you go from 50 clients to 80, the same team either drowns or you hire. OpsCopilot lets the same 3 people handle 80 clients.
❝ We don't have time to set up a new system ❞
You do not set it up — we do. I will sit with your team for 2 hours in Week 1. We add all your clients, configure templates, and run through one document request together. Your team runs it independently after that.
